THE AMAZING

A Project by Tim Holman & Claudio Guglieri

this on going project compiles patterns shared by
the most talented designers out there for you
to use freely in your designs.

WANT TO SHARE YOUR OWN PATTERNS?

Send us your site url, twitter name and your tileable pattern
and we will add it to the collection!
